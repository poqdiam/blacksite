Your Rights Under CCPA / CPRA

Right to Know
You have the right to know what personal information is collected about you, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.
Right to Access
You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Use the export button below to submit a request.
Right to Correct
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information. Update your profile information via My Profile.
Right to Delete
You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Note that compliance audit records may be retained per legal obligations even after a deletion request is honored.
Right to Opt-Out of Sale
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. No opt-out action is required.

Export My Data

Request Data Deletion
